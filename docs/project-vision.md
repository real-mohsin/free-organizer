# Free Organizer Vision

## Mission

Free Organizer aims to become the leading open-source business management platform for freelancers.

Many freelancers rely on multiple disconnected tools—spreadsheets, note-taking apps, invoicing software, calendars, and time zone converters—to manage their work. This fragmentation makes it difficult to understand the health of their business.

Free Organizer brings these workflows together into one lightweight, self-hostable application.

## Core Principles

- Open Source
- Privacy First
- Lightweight
- Fast
- Easy to Self-Host
- Community Driven

## Our Goals

- Help freelancers organize their business.
- Provide meaningful business insights, not just data.
- Maintain a simple and intuitive user experience.
- Build a project that developers enjoy contributing to.

## Long-Term Vision

Free Organizer should grow from a freelancer management tool into a complete business operating system for freelancers, consultants, and small agencies while remaining free, open source, and community-driven.
